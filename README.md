AI Employee - Smart AI-Powered Store Management System

AI Employee is an intelligent AI-powered web application that helps shop owners automate day-to-day business operations while providing customers with a smart shopping experience. The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, automation, inventory management, billing, analytics, and customer support into one modern web application.

🚀 Project Overview

Managing a retail store manually is time-consuming and often leads to mistakes in inventory, billing, customer support, and sales analysis.

AI Employee acts like a virtual employee that works 24/7, helping both shop owners and customers through AI-driven automation.

Whether it is answering customer questions, managing products, generating invoices, tracking orders, or analyzing business performance, AI Employee simplifies everything from one dashboard.
